    Previous survey links

    Get started with linking previous survey results

    Linking this survey to a previous one lets last time’s scores appear as trend lines — and keeps a team’s history even when groups are renamed, merged, or split. On this page you match the groups from both surveys: we suggest the links, you confirm them.

    Linking

    Match groups across both surveys

    Choose which survey to use as the previous one, then link this survey’s groups to its groups. That way you can still compare results, even when your organization has changed.

    Results

    See previous survey scores in your results

    In the results dashboard, every group shows its score from the previous survey — so you can quickly see what’s changed and where action is needed.

    Trends

    See trends in your dashboard

    When that previous survey is also linked to the one before it, you get a trend per question or theme — so you can follow each group’s longer-term direction.

    Comparing your survey results

    We’ve auto-selected the most recent completed survey to compare against. Its scores appear next to this survey’s, so you can see how each group has changed. Change it if you’d rather compare with another survey.
